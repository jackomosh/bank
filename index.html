The Power of Community Blood

Understanding why your donation matters more than you think.

🩸

Why Donation is Critical

Blood cannot be manufactured. It can only come from generous donors. With a shelf life of just 42 days, hospitals require a constant, daily supply to treat trauma patients, perform surgeries, and support cancer treatments.

🧬

The Danger of Relative Donors

While it seems safer to get blood from a close relative, medical science prefers strangers. Directed donations from first-degree relatives carry a high risk of TA-GVHD, a fatal condition where the donor's immune cells attack the recipient's body. A diverse community pool is medically safer.

🤝

Community Reliance

By building a network of localized, ready-to-help strangers, we ensure that the right blood type is always nearby. Your single donation can save up to three lives in your own neighborhood.
